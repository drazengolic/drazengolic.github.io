About this site

Hello, and welcome to my blog where I'll be writing about my tech ventures - both small and large, but also about stuff that I'm interested in in general. This is both a learning experience and applying of what I already know.

The blog will mostly be centered around general problem-solving, and functional-first programming languages such as F# and in a lesser extent OCaml.

You won't be able to comment, but I do welcome quality feedback nevertheless. If you do have one, feel free to email me at dgolic@gmail.com

I won't store any cookies, nor I will deploy any kind of analytics to this blog. I have no interest in such. Any number of messages I receive as a result of this blog will be the only measure of how good or bad it is.

Disclaimer: Opinions expressed on this site are exclusively my own and not of my current or previous employer(s).
